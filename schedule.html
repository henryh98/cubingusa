Below is the schedule for US Nationals 2016.

This year we will have four colored stages: Red, Blue, Green, and Orange.  We will also have a Yellow Room for longer events.  Approximately one month before the competition, we will post heat schedules for each competitor, indicating when and where they should compete in each event.  Please make sure you go to the right stage when it is your turn to compete.

Competitors need to check in at the registration desk before their first event.  We encourage competitors to check in during the times shown on the schedule, as we will have more staff working at the registration desk during those times.  However, you can check in any time during the day.  You only need to check in once during the competition.
